Neural Network Technical Analysis

Technical analysis is utilized to find trading opportunities in the stock market. It is based on information such as price movement, volume, and historical patterns.

This project will take advantage of the neural network's ability to identify patterns and recognize potential trading opportunities based on technical analysis.

How It Works

Instead of having a human read charts, the neural network will be trained to recognize patterns in stock price charts. It will return to the user a percentage resemblance of a stock pattern to a trained technical pattern, acting as a copilot for a day trader.

Note: This is a pattern-matching application, not a price prediction tool.

Goals

  • Students will learn how to clean and prepare datasets.
  • Train and deploy a neural network to detect technical patterns.
  • Gain an understanding of day trading and technical analysis.
